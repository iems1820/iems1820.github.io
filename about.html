History Of The School

Ispat English Medium School, the only ICSE Institution under SAIL, was started in 1961 with a modest strength of 27 students, 5 teachers and two classrooms. It was like a dream come true. A long awaited and desired need for an English medium School at Rourkela was fulfilled.

From such humble beginning, the school grew in stature and strength under the able guidance of the principals and the faculty members to be reckoned today as one of the reputed institutions of the state with about fourteen students hailing from different social background and sixty capable teachers busy shaping the destiny of the students.

In 1982 the institution embarked upon a new venture and started a Junior College (ISC+2) with science stream.
